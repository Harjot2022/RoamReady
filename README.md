RoamReady is a comprehensive travel companion designed to simplify trip planning, packing, and exploration. Whether you're a digital nomad or a weekend warrior, RoamReady ensures you have everything you need to embark on your next adventure with confidence.

🚀 Features
Trip Itinerary Planner: Organize your daily activities, flights, and hotel bookings in one place.

Smart Packing Lists: Generate automated packing lists based on your destination's weather and trip duration.

Budget Tracker: Keep an eye on your expenses in real-time to stay within your travel budget.

Offline Maps & Guides: Access critical travel information even without an internet connection.

Safety Alerts: Get real-time updates on local weather and travel advisories.
